Places to go and things to see!

Copenhagen is home to many beautiful sights and tourists atractions! Worried about cost? Well with the purchase of a Copenhagen card, depending on how many hours you purchase(72 hours= 124 USD),you can save a good amount of money and visit up to 83 attractions with no extra cost! Here are 4 places of interest to get you started.

events

  • Copenhagen
    Zoo

    For the Animal Lovers

    The Copenhagen Zoo has over 4,000 animals, from pandas to polar bears! Then visit our resturants and marvel and the unique architecture. Copenhagen Zoo is easily one of the most popular attractions in Denmark!

  • The National
    Museum

    For the Art Lovers

    There are many key exhibitions to enjoy at the National Museum, including Viking treasures and Egyptian mummies! If you have little one with you there is also the Children's Museum to check out!

  • Tivoli
    Gardens

    A Wonder for the Eyes

    The young and old alike can get the most out of the Tivoli Gardens! This international attraction inspired the likes of Walt Disney and Has Christian Anderson. Plenty of music, rides, and all in all fun for the whole family!

    Mermaid

    Scultpure with a Story

    Gifted to the city from Danish brewer Carl Jacobsen, this beutiful sculpture is located at Langelinie Peir and is known to be one of Copenhagens most iconic attractions!
