Welcome To Copenhagen

Copenhagen is the capital of Denmark and is home to a rich culture and vibrant populace. With plenty to do such as admire the architecture, learn about royal history, taste the delicious cuisine and many more, its no wonder that Copenhagen is one of the most popular places to visit! Even if you have your fill of the city itself, theres also a great experience to be had with exploring beyond the city limits such as traveling along the coast and the country side. Plan your trip today and have a fanstastic stay!

Of course traveling the city and viewing the sights are fantastic on its own, but where can find a place to eat? Well the city has no shortage of bistros and Michelin-starred restaurants to sate your hunger! From the local Nordic cuisine thatyou no doudt will come to love, there is also many resturants that bring in unique tastes from all over the world! Of course after a hefty meal you'll want to unwind with a cold bevrage, and rest assured you will have a long list of bars and cafes to try out, so take your time and taste the city!

Costs are always a worry to consider whever thinking about traveling. Luckily Copenhagen has a way to cut down on your costs while you ar here! The Copenhagen Card allows you to gain free access to a plethora of museums and other places of intrest. It also provides free public transportation within the region and discounts on resturants, cafesm and many more! The card can me purchased on our website and the cost varies depending on how many hours you plan on staying with us. Always be sure to make the most of your vacations!
